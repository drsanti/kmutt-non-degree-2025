Designing for Testability (DFT Principles)

Objective: Facilitate efficient electrical and functional testing during production and troubleshooting.

üîπ Test Point Planning

Concepts:

  • Add accessible test pads for power, ground, and signals.
  • Ensure probe accessibility for bed-of-nails or flying probe testers.
  • Use test pads >1 mm and labeled nets.

Example: An MCU board lacked test pads for UART interface ‚Äî debugging required manual soldering. Next revision included labeled test pads, saving 1 hour per board during QA.

üîπ Boundary Scan and JTAG

Use Cases:

  • For dense PCBs where direct probing isn‚Äôt possible.
  • Enables digital testing of interconnects and IC pin states.
  • Common for processors, FPGAs, and CPLDs.

üîπ Built-In Self-Test (BIST) and Diagnostics

üîπ Design for In-Circuit Testing (ICT)
